## 4th Wall Break — DECIDED ✅
**Date:** 2026-03-07

- Su WILL use 4th wall breaks in the film
- **Rule:** Su can glance at the camera (break the 4th wall) ONLY when other humans are present — auto drivers, studio guests, strangers
- **Rule:** Su does NOT break the 4th wall when he is alone — this preserves the "lyrical loneliness" quality of solo scenes
- **Rationale:** Su's face and eyes are his acting instrument. The glances create a public persona (comedic observer) vs private persona (lonely, internal). The contrast IS the point
- The 4th wall break with auto drivers works especially well — Su observes their behavior (staring at girls, breaking signals, etc.) and his reaction to camera tells the audience everything without dialogue
