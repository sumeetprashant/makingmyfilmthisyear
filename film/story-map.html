Act One

The Comedian Is Here

Daily rhythm. Three studio nights. One portal opens.

What happens

A girl — around 8 years old — is walking through a misty, uncertain place. She wears adult sunglasses that are slightly too big for her face. She's not scared. She's curious. A cat named Mau appears, running on two legs (her back legs don't work in the world we haven't seen yet — but here, she just runs). The cat leads the girl forward. The mist parts for them. A soft meow. Then, cut to black. Title: HOW TO NOT MAKE FILMS.
